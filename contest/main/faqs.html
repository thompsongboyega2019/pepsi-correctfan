Picks & Tips

Consider these tips when you are making selections;

Goalkeepers and defenses who are facing powerhouse teams tend to get more opportunities for saves (for GK) and interceptions and clearances (for DEF). Look for bargains on value defenses, and go big on attackers.
Midfielders are the most dependable source for high-upside performances. So, pick players who fill the stat sheet, even if they aren’t goal scorers — then look for them to have a big match if they do put one in the net.
Forwards are the most boom-or-bust performers, since most of their points will come from goals. If you’re entering multiple lineups, consider using different combinations of forwards to expand your likelihood of picking up a hot scorer.
Availabilitypercentage indicator quickly tells you if a player is expected to start and if the player is confirmed to start. So,there is no guessing if a player is going to ride the bench or start a game.
